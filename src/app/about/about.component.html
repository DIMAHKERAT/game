ABOUT

This game is made to help students or any interested person to learn a fundamental basic of propositional logic (SAT).

HOW TO PLAY

There are two colors, winning color and losing color. And there are symbols. For each, an inverted symbol can exist. The Symbols are divided in boxes.

The player should assign the two colors to each symbol such that each symbol and its invert-symbol have a different color.

The player wins if in each box at least one symbol in the winning color.
